About me

Since my youth I'm interested in designing & programming. I wanted to create my own video games instead of playing them. In my free time I used every minute to teach myself new skills. With the age of 18 I finally made this passion to my profession. Since then I have been trying to find the right solution for each customer's requirement. Every project is done with my passion for programming. No matter if it is an online shop with 10,000 articles or just an HTML newsletter.

In 2019 I founded the Digital Agency MBM&CO in Frankfurt.

Services

Webdesign

I create you a design which will perfectly fit every screen size. I look for current trends and the needs of your target group.

Programming

The design will be implemented with the latest web techniques. I pay attention to a clean code and an optimal fit on all devices.

On-Page SEO

I optimze the code for search engines during the programming. I will also add structured data fo a better presentation in Google & Co.

Off-Page SEO

I will help you to find the right keywords for your website and create landing pages for the keywords. I also support you in creating ads with Google Adwords.

Social-Media

I will advise you which social media channels are the best choice for you and integrate them with your website. In addition, I support you in the planning and implementation of advertisements.

Photography & Video Production

A good design also includes impressive pictures & videos. Whether team photos or drone shots of your company building, I'll be happy to help.
